## Consistent Style Direction (apply to every prompt)

> Vibrant flat vector cartoon illustration with bold thick black outlines, limited 4-color palette dominated by warm gold, cream, deep charcoal, and forest green. Slight cel-shading with single light source. Stylized like a modern streetwear brand mascot — think a cross between South Park geometric simplicity, BAPE's Baby Milo cleanness, and Adult Swim's confident linework. Background is solid flat color or a simple radial/gradient panel — never photo-realistic. Always keep the **Hoodlums Country Club skull-cowboy mascot** recognizable across the series: a grinning cartoon skull wearing a cowboy hat, with a gold tooth, a small bandana around the jaw, and a confident posture. Hand-drawn imperfections welcome.
